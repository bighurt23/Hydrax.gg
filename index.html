How to read your mining numbers

Profitable mining comes down to three levers: revenue (coin price × your share of the network), power cost (watts × your electricity rate), and hardware payback (how fast the rig earns back what you paid). Get those three right and everything else is noise.

Why electricity rate matters most

At $0.12/kWh a 1,200 W rig burns about $3.46/day in power before it earns a cent. Drop to $0.06/kWh and you've nearly doubled your margin without touching the hardware. Run the Electricity Cost tab above to find your own break-even power price.

Don't forget difficulty growth

Network difficulty tends to climb, which slowly shrinks your share of block rewards. The ROI calculator lets you model that so your payback estimate isn't fantasy.
